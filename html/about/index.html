About Me

We Should Talk

You can email me at alexlande@gmail.com to shoot the breeze, ask a question, or talk about working together.

Hi. I'm a web designer based in Boulder, Colorado since 2007.

I went to school at the University of Colorado, where I studied anthropology. I have an unfortunately large collection of (mostly) punk rock records, have studied mantled howling monkeys in Costa Rica, and have a vague plan to climb every mountain in Colorado over 14,000 feet.

Nowadays, I create striking, accessible, and future friendly websites, ranging from online shops to professional learning systems. If you'd like, you can get in touch by email or find me on Twitter, GitHub, or Zerply.
